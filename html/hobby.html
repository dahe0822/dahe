Miniature

Making miniature is my oldest hobby.
When I was grade 3, I came across miniature in my textbook.
It was very interesting to me from the moment I saw.
I even sold my miniature works on the internet when I was younger.
Although making miniatures is a super delicate hobby and took a lot of my time,
I love making miniatures because it makes me feel like creating another world.
I wonder how it would turn out when it meets engineering.
I someday want to make a little robot and let it live in my miniature world.

Baking

Baking is one of my old hobbies.
I started baking when I was about grade six.
Baking helped me to grow my patience.
There is a high chance to fail in baking; one little problem would cause a big failure.
or I have to spend the whole day to satisfy myself with the outcome;
I might have to repeat the same process over and over again due to a little problem.
I enjoy baking because of the pleasure of sharing.
As sharing my cookies, cakes, and bread as a gift with the people around me,
I was able to show my gratitude and see people smile.
It is one of the reasons why I feel baking is very addictive.
