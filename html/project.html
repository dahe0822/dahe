Life of Teddy is a AI game I created in July, 2016.
A player becomes a friend and creater of a teddybear and will give a soul and life to the teddybear, named "Teddy".
I made this game to achieve a basic AI game.
Also, I wanted to make the player realize how important it is to be consistent in giving love
to things or someone you decided to love.

'Jack the Hopper' is a game to challenge to explore the world beyond the sky.
Jack who is the charactor in the game wants to reach as high as possible.
His dream is to reach the Exosphere layer.
He climbed up a mountain to start his journey.
This game is made to help him to achieve his dream.
How to play
The black bar should not miss him and should touch him to make him hop.
If you miss him, then he will fall all the way down he came up.
There are some obstacles in the sky and if he touches them, he loses a heart.
If you leave him for days,
so that his satisfaction reaches 0,
as I warned in the begining,
he loses his soul and life.

'Earnest Jellyfish' is my first game ever I created.
This is basically a different version of 'Flappy Bird"
yet I wanted to differenciate it in terms of graphics and story.
The jellyfish in the game wants to live a joyful and long enough life,
but fishermen have abandoned a number of nets in the ocean.
This is threatening lives in the ocean,
especially this jellyfish just started to enjoy his life.
He does not want to end his life that quickly.
The player's task is to help this jellyfish to enjoy his life.
Let's begin
When you goes over score 100,
you will recieve jellyfish's will
appreciating your effort to help him to live longer and enjoyable life.
